DialogSum Challenge

DialogSum is a shared task on summarizing real-life scenario dialogues, accepted by INLG 2021, to encourage researchers to address challenges in dialogue summarization from multiple aspects. DialogSum Challenge Paper (INLG 2021)

DialogSum Dataset

DialogSum is a manually annotated large-scale dataset for dialogue summariation. Different from previous dialogue summarization dataset, DialogSum focuses on dialogues under rich real-life scenarios, including more diverse task-oriented dialogues. DialogSum Paper (Findings of ACL 2021)
